Conveyor Screw

Our conveyor screws play a pivotal role in efficiently transporting bulk materials from one point to another, ensuring a continuous and reliable material flow in various industries, including mining, agriculture, food processing, and cement plants. With Vikas Kontrols' expertise in design and manufacturing, these conveyor screws guarantee smooth handling and seamless material transfer, meeting the industrial demands for enhanced productivity.

Rotary Coarse Feeder

Vikas Kontrols offers rotary coarse feeders, essential for regulating the flow of coarse-grained materials, optimizing production efficiency, and reducing material wastage. These feeders find extensive application in industries like cement, mining, power generation, and chemicals, where precise and controlled feeding of coarse materials is crucial for seamless operations.

Rotary Airlock Feeder

Coarse feeders RAL 630 & 1000 (Rotary Air Lock) offered by Vikas Kontrols are critical components for efficient and controlled material discharge in bulk handling systems. Industries in power plants, cement manufacturing, and chemical processing utilize these feeders to achieve reliable and continuous discharge of coarse materials from hoppers and silos, enhancing overall system productivity.

Actuator

Our actuator without drive is an indispensable component that allows for controlled movements of equipment and machinery without the need for external power sources. Widely used in industrial automation, Vikas Kontrols' actuators ensure precise control and positioning of valves, dampers, and other equipment, enhancing operational efficiency and safety across various industries.

IVC Damper

The IVC dampers, available in open and closed types, play a critical role in regulating gas flow within industrial processes, ensuring optimal system performance. Industries such as power generation, steel manufacturing, waste incineration, and HVAC systems rely on Vikas Kontrols' IVC dampers to achieve precise control over airflow, pressure, and temperature, thereby enhancing operational efficiency and safety.

LUMP_BREAKER

The lump breakers offered by Vikas Kontrols are crucial for reducing large chunks or agglomerates of materials into smaller, more manageable particles, improving material handling and processing. Industries in fertilizer production, mineral processing, and recycling rely on these breakers to break down oversized materials for further processing and efficient handling.

Actuators Pre-Assembly & Assembled

Our actuators pre-assembly & assembled components provide essential mechanical motion for valves, dampers, and other equipment, facilitating seamless automation and control. Industries in oil and gas, water treatment, and manufacturing utilize Vikas Kontrols' actuators to ensure precise movement and positioning, enhancing process efficiency and reliability.

Dozzing_Valve

Vikas Kontrols' dozzing valves with actuators ensure precise and automated dosing of materials, guaranteeing accurate and consistent feed rates. Industries in chemical processing, pharmaceuticals, and food production rely on these valves to achieve optimal material dosing, reducing waste, and enhancing overall production quality.

Paddle Mixture

Vikas Kontrols' paddle mixtures are versatile blending devices that efficiently mix bulk materials, ensuring homogeneity and consistent product quality. Industries in pharmaceuticals, food processing, and chemicals utilize these mixers to achieve efficient blending of powders, granules, and other materials, meeting specific production requirements.

Screw Sampler Assembly

Our screw sampler assemblies are essential for obtaining representative samples of bulk materials, facilitating accurate analysis and quality control. Mining, cement production, and chemical industries utilize Vikas Kontrols' screw samplers to ensure reliable and consistent sample collection for rigorous testing and compliance with quality standards.
