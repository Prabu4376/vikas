Metallurgy Consultant

Product 1

Process metallurgy of steels by forging, casting, welding, and various heat treatment processes. The breadth of coverage and the numerous examples provide an invaluable resource for the designer, engineer, metallurgist, mechanical and materials engineers, quality control technicians, and heat treaters. Potential failures of heat treated steel are detailed to help identify cause in preventing future occurrence and improving reliability of heat treating process and part performance.

With a detailed focus on steel failures, this work can help identify root causes of failure, even if a thermal process is not specifically being considered as cause. Potential failures are covered with respect to design, material composition, component production, and thermal processing. Beginning with design aspects of component failure, chapters are devoted to several aspects of steel manufacturing including casting, forging or powder metallurgy in addition to heat treating operations. Topics include production problems such as porosity, flaws, and surface defect that may influence failures occurring during subsequent heat treatment or during use. Many examples of heat treatment failures are provided with special focus on the demands of tool steels and aerospace materials.

For over 40 years I’ve been providing metallurgical engineering and materials engineering expertise for engineering failure analysis. I help clients understand how failures occurred, determine their root cause, and determine how to prevent them from reoccurring.

Failure analysis + design and manufacturing expertise I understand the pressure to get the information needed to make decisions about failures and manufacturing quality problems. Furthermore, my broad experience with product design, manufacturing, and root cause analysis enables me to bring multiple perspectives to help you quickly solve problems. You can count on me to…

  • Determine the metallurgical analyses required to get the information you need.
  • Work with you to identify the root cause of the failure.
  • Communicate findings so you’ll understand what went wrong and how to fix it.
  • Help you determine corrective actions.
  • Failure analysis and root cause analysis experience
  • I have experience with a wide variety of items including consumer products, machinery, industrial equipment, medical devices, electronics, plumbing, and hardware. Also, I help manufacturing companies determine the root cause of manufacturing quality problems and defects.
