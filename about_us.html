ABOUT US

Vikas Kontrols boasts a rich legacy of over four decades, earning a renowned reputation as a top-notch designer and manufacturer of premium bulk material handling equipment in Chennai,India. Our extensive range of offerings includes conveyor screws, rotary coarse feeders, actuator without drive, IVC Dampers, and rotary airlock feeders, along with other exceptional products such as Dozzing Valve with Actuators, IVC Dampers (both open and closed types), Screw Sampler Assembly, Paddle Mixture, Lump Breaker, Actuators Pre-Assembly & Assembled, Coarse Feeders, and RAL 630 & 1000 (Rotary Air Lock). These state-of-the-art products are specifically designed to meet the needs of cement and mineral industries, catering to esteemed clients such as JSW Steel, Tata Steel, L&T ECC Division, Grasim Industries,Ambuja Cements, Kesoram Industries, India Cements, Ramco Cements, and Chettinadu Cements.

We have established an excellent reputation in the field of bulk material handling equipment, consistently meeting and exceeding our clients' expectations. Our dedication to delivering exceptional products tailored to their unique requirements has made us a trusted partner in their operations. We have also expanded our reach beyond India, exporting our high-quality equipment to countries like Malaysia.

In our pursuit of continuous growth and innovation, we have forged a strategic collaboration with the esteemed German company, MLZ GmbH. This collaboration enables us to manufacture and supply Motorized Linear Actuators, incorporating cutting-edge technology and expertise. While the core elements of our equipment are sourced from Germany and Austria, we take pride in manufacturing all other components in India. This ensures that our products meet the highest standards of quality and reliability, as per the specifications and guidelines set forth by our collaborators.

At our core, we are driven by a passion for excellence and a commitment to customer satisfaction. With our extensive industry experience, unwavering dedication, and a wide range of superior products, we stand as a reliable partner in the bulk material handling industry. We look forward to serving you and meeting your specific requirements with our exceptional equipment and services.

Please feel free to reach out to us for any inquiries or further information.
